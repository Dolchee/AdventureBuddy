How It Works

Upon clicking "Get Started" at the bottom of this box, we will ask to aquire your location information.

This information is passed to our servers along with your full name and phone number that you should enter into our "Sign In" box.

From there, your location is updated every 30 seconds on our servers and if it stops moving for a period of 10 minutes a prompt will pop up asking you if you're OK. If so, just hit "Yes, I'm OK" and the prompt will disappear.

If no user input is registered within 2 minutes, emergency serivces will be sent your:

Name
Phone Number
and Geolocation coordinates

The emergency services will use this info to track your location and find you A.S.A.P!

Enjoy our easy to use app and trek on with your personal "AdventureBuddy!"
